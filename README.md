# HabitZen

Welcome to **HabitZen**, a minimalist yet powerful habit tracking application designed to help you build routines, monitor your daily progress, and achieve your personal goals.

Built with Next.js, React, and Tailwind CSS, HabitZen offers a clean, intuitive, and responsive user experience.

## Features

- **Create & Manage Habits:** Easily add, edit, and delete your daily habits.
- **Daily Tracking:** Mark habits as complete for the day with a simple checkbox.
- **Calendar View:** Visualize your consistency and progress over time with a color-coded calendar.
- **AI-Powered Suggestions:** Get intelligent recommendations for new habits based on your existing ones and your stated goals.
- **Custom Reminders:** Set daily notifications to never miss a habit.
- **Persistent Storage:** Your habits and progress are saved locally in your browser, so your data is always available.
- **Responsive Design:** Works beautifully on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

## Getting Started

The main application logic resides in `src/app/page.tsx`. From there, you can explore the various components in `src/components/` and the AI logic in `src/ai/`.
